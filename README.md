# Hardware Shop Management System

The Hardware Shop Management System is a desktop/web application developed to automate the daily operations of a hardware store. It provides an easy-to-use interface for managing inventory, recording purchases, maintaining customer information, and generating sales invoices.

## Features

- 📦 Stock/Inventory Management
- 🛒 Purchase Management
- 👥 Customer Management
- 🧾 Bill & Invoice Generation
- 🔍 Product Search
- 📊 Stock Availability Tracking
- 💰 Sales Management
- 📈 Reports and Transaction History
- 🔐 Secure Login System (if implemented)

## Benefits

- Reduces manual paperwork
- Tracks stock in real time
- Simplifies billing and sales
- Organizes customer records
- Improves business efficiency
